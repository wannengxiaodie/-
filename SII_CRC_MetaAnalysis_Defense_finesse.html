Prognostic Value of the Systemic Immune-Inflammation Index for Colorectal Cancer.系统免疫炎症指数对结直肠癌预后价值的研究

A pooled analysis of 35 studies and 26,812 patients clarifies where SII predicts survival, and where it does not.汇集 35 项研究、26,812 例患者，明确 SII 在哪些场景下可预测生存，哪些场景下价值有限。

The Clinical Challenge.临床挑战与问题

Colorectal cancer remains a leading cause of cancer-related mortality worldwide. TNM staging alone cannot account for the wide inter-individual variability in prognosis, and systemic inflammation is now understood to drive tumor progression, metastasis, and treatment resistance.结直肠癌仍是全球癌症相关死亡的主要原因之一。仅靠 TNM 分期难以解释个体间广泛的预后差异，而系统性炎症已被证实驱动肿瘤进展、转移和治疗耐药。

Existing inflammatory markers such as NLR and PLR show promise but capture only part of the inflammatory-immune axis. The Systemic Immune-Inflammation Index (SII) combines three compartments into a single composite read. Prior SII studies in CRC, however, report inconsistent findings due to methodological heterogeneity and undefined cut-off values.NLR、PLR 等已有炎症指标具有一定价值，但仅反映了炎症-免疫轴的部分维度。系统免疫炎症指数（SII）将三大血细胞整合为单一复合指标。然而，既往 CRC 中 SII 研究因方法学异质性和临界值不统一，结论存在分歧。

SII · Composite Index复合指数
SII = Platelet × Neutrophil血小板 × 中性粒细胞 Lymphocyte淋巴细胞

Knowledge Gap知识缺口

  • Optimal SII cut-off values remain undefined across the literature.文献中 SII 最佳临界值尚未统一。
  • Prognostic value varies by treatment modality, TNM stage, and tumor location.预后价值因治疗方式、TNM 分期和肿瘤部位而异。
  • No comprehensive meta-analysis with sufficient subgroup granularity exists.尚无具备足够亚组颗粒度的全面 Meta 分析。
  • The most recent prior analysis (Tan et al., 2025) was limited to 27 studies, searched to March 2024.最新既往分析（Tan 等，2025）仅纳入 27 项研究，检索至 2024 年 3 月。

Study Objectives.研究目标

Primary Objective主要目标

To clarify the prognostic value of SII for overall survival (OS) and progression-free survival (PFS) in colorectal cancer through a comprehensive meta-analysis.通过全面的 Meta 分析，明确 SII 对结直肠癌总生存期（OS）和无进展生存期（PFS）的预后价值。

Secondary Objectives次要目标

  • Explore heterogeneity sources via subgroup analyses: TNM stage, tumor location, treatment, geography, timing of SII measurement.通过亚组分析探索异质性来源：TNM 分期、肿瘤部位、治疗方式、地域、SII 检测时机。
  • Assess whether variability in SII cut-off value moderates prognostic estimates via meta-regression.通过 Meta 回归评估 SII 临界值差异是否影响预后效应量。
  • Compare findings with previous meta-analyses and provide updated evidence through November 2025.与既往 Meta 分析比较，提供截至 2025 年 11 月的最新证据。

Inclusion Criteria纳入标准

  • Surgery, chemotherapy, or combined treatment.接受手术、化疗或联合治疗。

  • Hazard ratio with 95% CI available or derivable.提供或可推导风险比（HR）及 95% 置信区间。

SII & Overall Survival.SII 与总生存期

2.11HR · pooled合并
Elevated SII is significantly associated with worse overall survival in colorectal cancer. SII 升高与结直肠癌更差的总生存期显著相关。 95% CI 1.73 – 2.57 · p < 0.00001 · 32 cohorts个队列 · 25,366 patients例患者 · I² = 92%

Other Moderators其他调节变量

Age (≥65 vs <65) emerged as a significant moderator for OS (p = 0.041), with stronger effect in elderly populations. This may reflect cumulative inflammatory burden with aging.年龄（≥65 vs <65）是 OS 的显著调节因素（p = 0.041），老年人群效应更强。这可能反映随年龄增长累积的炎症负荷。

Publication year, sample size, and geographic region did not reach statistical significance as moderators.发表年份、样本量和地域作为调节因素均未达统计学意义。

Biological Mechanisms.生物学机制

Neutrophilia中性粒细胞升高

  • Release of VEGF & MMPs释放 VEGF 和 MMP
  • Promote angiogenesis促进血管生成
  • Facilitate metastasis辅助转移

Thrombocytosis血小板增多

  • Platelet-tumor aggregates血小板-肿瘤聚集
  • Protect circulating tumor cells保护循环肿瘤细胞
  • Enhance tumor cell survival增强肿瘤细胞存活

Lymphocytopenia淋巴细胞减少

  • Impaired CD8+ T-cell functionCD8+ T 细胞功能受损
  • Reduced antitumor immunity抗肿瘤免疫降低
  • Treg & MDSC infiltrationTreg 和 MDSC 浸润
The combined effect of elevated neutrophils and platelets with reduced lymphocytes creates a pro-tumor microenvironment. SII captures all three compartments in a single composite index. 中性粒细胞、血小板升高伴淋巴细胞减少的联合效应构建了促肿瘤微环境。SII 将三个维度整合为单一复合指标。

Clinical Implications.临床意义

Proposed Clinical Framework建议的临床应用框架

  • Baseline SII at diagnosis for risk stratification.诊断时基线 SII 用于风险分层。
  • Serial SII monitoring every 3 months.每 3 个月动态监测 SII。
  • Integrate with TNM staging, especially in Stage III.与 TNM 分期整合，尤其 III 期。
  • Treatment intensification if SII persistently elevated.SII 持续升高者考虑强化治疗。
Stage III shows the strongest association (OS HR 3.65). Post-treatment SII (OS HR 2.44 vs 1.98) supports dynamic monitoring. Bevacizumab attenuates the signal, suggesting anti-inflammatory properties. III 期关联最强（OS HR 3.65）。治疗后 SII（OS HR 2.44 vs 1.98）支持动态监测。贝伐珠单抗减弱效应，提示其抗炎特性。

Limitations.研究局限性

01

Cut-off Heterogeneity临界值异质性

SII cut-off values ranged from 340 to 1,505, limiting cross-study comparability.SII 临界值范围 340–1,505，限制了研究间可比性。

02

Study Design Imbalance研究设计失衡

38 retrospective vs 2 prospective cohorts; prospective data underpowered.38 项回顾性 vs 2 项前瞻性队列；前瞻性数据效力不足。

03

Geographic Bias地域偏倚

Most studies from Asia; findings may not generalize to non-Asian populations.多数研究来自亚洲；结论可能不适用于非亚洲人群。

04

Tumor Location Granularity肿瘤部位颗粒度不足

Limited data on right-sided vs left-sided CRC differences.右半与左半结肠癌差异数据有限。

05

High Heterogeneity高异质性

I² = 92% for OS and 73% for PFS warrants cautious interpretation.OS I² = 92%，PFS I² = 73%，需谨慎解读。

Conclusions & Future Directions.结论与未来方向

Conclusions结论

  • Elevated pre-treatment SII predicts worse OS (HR 2.11) and PFS (HR 2.16).治疗前 SII 升高预测更差的 OS（HR 2.11）和 PFS（HR 2.16）。
  • SII is a robust, accessible prognostic biomarker from routine blood counts.SII 是一种稳健、可及的预后生物标志物，来自常规血常规。
  • Strongest in Stage III disease (OS HR 3.65).III 期疾病中效应最强（OS HR 3.65）。
  • Post-treatment SII may offer superior prognostic precision.治疗后 SII 可能提供更优的预后精准度。

Future Research未来研究方向

  • Large-scale prospective multicenter studies with standardized protocols.标准化方案的大规模前瞻性多中心研究。
  • ROC-based validation of optimal SII cut-off values.基于 ROC 的 SII 最佳临界值验证。
  • Integration with molecular subtyping (MSI, KRAS, BRAF).与分子分型（MSI、KRAS、BRAF）整合。
  • Development of nomograms for personalized prognostication.开发个体化预后列线图。
